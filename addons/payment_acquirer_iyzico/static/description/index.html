Configuration

  • 1- From Accounting: -> Configuraiton -> Payment Acquires
  • 2- From payment acquirers list, choose “Iyzico payment”
  • 3- Enter Iyzico Credentials and other configuration (journal, Messages that wants the customer recieved after finished the payment operation)

Is this app compatible with Harpiya Enterprise as well as Community?

Yes, the app works perfectly well with Harpiya Enterprise (On-premise and Harpiya.SH) as well as Community. Harpiya Online (Cloud) does not allow installation of third-party apps and hence this app cannot be installed on Harpiya Online.

I need an extra feature in this app. How can I request it?

You can contact us to request a customization in the app.

Do I get free upgrades and support if I purchase the app?

If you purchase our app for Harpiya Version 12 and prior, we will provide you access to all the versions up to 12 depending on their availability. Starting version 13, every version of the app will be sold separately.

We offer free support for a duration of 90 days from the date of purchase. Kindly read our support policy to know the details.
